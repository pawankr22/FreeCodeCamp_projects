Let's play Rock, Paper, Scissors!

Rules to play the game.

Your opponent is your computer.

You have to choose between these 3 Rock, Paper, and Scissors.

Those who will score 3 point first will win.

How to earn points

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

If both players choose the same option, it is a tie and no one scores.
